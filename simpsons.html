"The Simpsons" Slippy Map

I designed this map for my Graphic Design in Cartography class at UW Madison when we were tasked with creating a "slippy" map with a certain artistic style, so I chose the art style of The Simpsons. Also as part of the assignment, we needed to include at least 25 points of interest relevant to our theme. On my map, the points of interest are places visited by the Simpson family and other characters on the show. I created this map using Mapbox Studio Classic.
