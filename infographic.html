Do More Bars Mean More Accidents?

I designed this infographic for my Graphic Design in Cartography class at UW Madison when we were tasked with creating an infographic that tells a visual story. For mine, I chose to investigate the relationship between the number of bars and number of alcohol involved car accidents (for every 10,000 people) in Wisconsin at the county level.
