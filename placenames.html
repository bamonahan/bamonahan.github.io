United States Place Names: the Good, the Bad, and the Deadly

I designed this infographic for my Graphic Design in Cartography class at UW Madison. The idea came from a combination of my fascination with place name origins, and my desire to make a map with "emotion based" design. This resulted in an infographic telling the stories of places with happy, sad, and frightening names. Place names were obtained using the USGS Geographic Names Information System.
